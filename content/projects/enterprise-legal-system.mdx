## The Challenge

The legal contract approval process at Telkomsigma was heavily reliant on manual paperwork and fragmented email chains. This caused significant delays, increased the risk of lost documents, and made it difficult for stakeholders to track the status of critical contracts.

## The Solution

We built a robust, enterprise-grade system to digitize the entire lifecycle of a legal contractâ€”from drafting and internal review to final approval and archival.

- **Workflow Engine**: Custom-built logic handling complex multi-tier approval hierarchies.
- **Asynchronous Processing**: Used **Celery** and **RabbitMQ** to handle resource-intensive tasks like PDF generation and email notifications without blocking the user interface.
- **Document Integration**: Seamless connection with **Microsoft SharePoint** via REST APIs for centralized, secure storage of all contract files.
